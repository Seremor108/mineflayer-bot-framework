# Mineflayer Bot Framework v1.4.0

Version 1.4.0 expands the autonomy service with four independently configurable behavior groups.

## Projectile dodging

The bot predicts whether arrows, spectral arrows, snowballs, eggs, and fireballs will cross its position. An incoming threat creates an emergency-priority task that can interrupt normal work. Dodge selection prefers lateral movement and rejects routes without a solid safe floor, preventing the bot from stepping toward the void or configured hazardous blocks.

## Animal interactions

When enabled, the bot can locate nearby unsheared sheep and cows. It equips shears to shear sheep or an empty bucket to milk cows, then restores its previously held item. Per-entity cooldowns prevent repeated interactions.

## Visible ore mining

When enabled and idle, the bot searches for configured ore blocks, filters the results to visible blocks, approaches the nearest candidate, equips a suitable harvest tool when available, and mines it. Revisit cooldowns avoid repeatedly selecting an unreachable block.

## Hunger-aware eating

When hunger falls below the configured threshold, the bot selects an allowed food item from its inventory and consumes it. Selection can prioritize higher food values. When no inventory food is available and cake eating is enabled, it searches for visible placed cake and eats slices until reaching the configured target or exhausting the cake.

## Configuration

See `config.example.json` under `plugins.autonomy` for:

- `projectileDodging`
- `eating`
- `animalInteractions`
- `visibleOreMining`

All existing chest-looting and inventory-disposal settings remain available.

## Validation

The v1.4 test suite covers projectile trajectory prediction, outgoing-projectile rejection, void-safe dodge selection, sheep state handling, animal filtering, visible ore and cake discovery, food scoring and selection, and independent configuration toggles.
